Two-Year Course for JEE Main & JEE Advanced
SKCCareer   offers
two
-year Courses for JEE Main and JEE Advanced
. Our courses are designed for students currently enrolled in class X who will enter class XI in 2023. XII Boards, other engineering entrance exams, JEE (Main & Advanced), and different international and national level Olympiads are all covered together.
Since 2018, the
Joint Entrance Examination (Advanced)
is now conducted online, using a computer-based test format. The only mode of administration for all next JEE Advanced exams would be computer-based testing (CBT).

JEE Main, which was previously administered by CBSE, is now being administered by the newly established National Testing Agency (NTA). For admissions in the following academic session, the JEE (Main) - 2022 will be held over the course of 02 (two) sessions. Thus, the following advantages will accrue to the candidates:
Droppers won't have to squander a whole year because of this, which will lower the likelihood of dropping a year.
In the event that they are unable to provide their utmost effort during the first attempt, this will give the applicants two chances to raise their exam marks.
Students will gain first-hand experience taking an exam on their first try, and they will learn from their mistakes so they may do better on their second try.
SKCCareer   assists you in preparing for JEE Main and JEE Advanced in a pattern-proof method. This indicates that, even if the exam's format is changed at the last minute, you are ready to offer your best and most effective performance. Only at our institute do students perform well in XII Board exams, other engineering entrance exams, and JEE Main & Advanced.

Benefits and features of the two year JEE main & JEE Advance course:
Video Recordings of Classes:
All the class videos are available for the students in SKCCareer   Mobile App. This way, they can access the recordings at any time and more thoroughly review any material.
Online Test Series:
Take practice tests for the JEE Main and JEE Advanced exams from the convenience of your home. Test analysis, Weak Areas Improvement, and Time Management are features. As JEE Main and JEE Advanced will be conducted entirely online in 2023, applicants must certainly complete online JEE practice exams.
National Benchmarking:
SKCCareer   offers competitiveness and analysis on a national scale via an internet platform. The testing will take place on the same platform with students from all around India and overseas (NRI students). Students will gain a better grasp of the level of competition and performance, so they may focus their efforts on improving their areas of weakness.
