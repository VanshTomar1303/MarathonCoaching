IIT JEE Class 11 Syllabus
The IIT JEE Class 11 Syllabus covers all the important chapters from Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. The topics included in the IIT JEE Class 11 Syllabus are:
IIT JEE Class 11 Physics Syllabus:
Units and Measurements
Kinematics
Laws of Motion
Work, Energy, and Power
Rotational Motion
Gravitation
Properties of Solids and Liquids
Thermodynamics
Transfer of Heat
Wave Optics
Electric Charges and Fields
Current Electricity
Magnetic Effects of Current and Magnetism
Electromagnetic Induction and Alternating Currents
Electromagnetic Waves
Dual Nature of Matter and Radiation
Atoms and Nuclei
Electronic Devices
Communication Systems
IIT JEE Class 11 Chemistry Syllabus:
Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry
States of Matter
Atomic Structure
Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure
Thermodynamics
Equilibrium
Redox Reactions and Electrochemistry
The s –Block Elements
General Principles and Processes of Isolation of Metals
p–Block Elements -Group 14 (Carbon Family)
Organic Chemistry–Some Basic Principles and Techniques and Hydrocarbons
Haloalkanes and Haloarenes
Alcohols, Phenols, and Ethers
Aldehydes, Ketones and Carboxylic Acids
Organic Compounds Containing Nitrogen
Biomolecules
Polymers
Chemistry in Everyday Life
IIT JEE Class 11 Mathematics Syllabus:
Sets, Relations, and Functions
Algebra
Coordinate Geometry
Calculus
Mathematical Reasoning
Statistics and Probability
These are the important topics that are included in the
IIT JEE Class 11 Syllabus
. Students must make sure that they cover all the topics in order to score well in the examination.
