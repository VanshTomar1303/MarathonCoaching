What is the IIT JEE Foundation Course and what are its benefits?
The
IIT JEE Foundation Course
is an online course offered by Marathon Academy. It aims to prepare students for the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE), which is the main entrance exam for engineering colleges in India.
The course covers all the important topics of the
IIT JEE syllabus
