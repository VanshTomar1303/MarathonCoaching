West Bengal State Board Exam Details
The state's primary educational board, the West Bengal Board, is broken up into various sections. The West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education, the West Bengal Board of Primary Education, and the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education are these organizations (WBCHSE). The West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE), founded in 1951, is in charge of administering the Class 10 board exam. It is also responsible for establishing the curriculum, textbooks, and syllabus for Classes 6 through 10. The board is also in charge of reviewing the board test papers and disseminating the exam's results.
Andhra Pradesh State Board Exam Details
The primary educational body in Andhra Pradesh is the Directorate of Government Examinations, also known as the Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education. The state's Class 10 board exams are administered by the Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (BSEAP), sometimes referred to as the AP SSC Board. The Andhra Intermediate or Class 12 exams are administered by the Board of Intermediate Education (BIE). Additionally, the AP Board is in charge of establishing the state's Class 1 through Class 12 student body's texts, curricula, and syllabi. The Class 10 and Class 12 Board Exam Evaluation and Results Announcement is another crucial duty of the AP Board.
Bihar State Board Exam Details
The primary educational authority in the state of Bihar is known as BSEB or the Bihar School Examination Board. Every year, all the schools connected with BSEB in Bihar must take the Class 10 (Matric) and Class 12 (Inter) exams. The Bihar board governs each of these tests.
Karnataka State Board Exam Details
The primary educational authority in Karnataka is the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board or KSEEB. The Karnataka board was created in 1966 and is in charge of conducting state-level exams like the SSLC and PUC. The Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board, or KSEEB, is responsible for regulating all aspects of schooling. We have attempted to cover the many duties and activities of the Karnataka Secondary Education Board on this page. A comprehensive choice of study materials, the Karnataka State Board syllabus, and other tools are also offered to aid students in getting ready for the Karnataka State Board exams. In India, the secondary school system is overseen and governed by the Karnataka State Examination Board.
